Welcome to Community on Campus: Palestinian Solidarity at UCLA!
This web-based tool is designed to document and amplify the voices and experiences of those involved in the Pro-Palestine movement on campus by providing a detailed view of various protest, encampment, and significant locations through a map and allowing you to explore survey responses and contribute to our ongoing conversation.

Here's how you can interact:
  • Use the buttons on the right of the map to view different types of locations.
  • Scroll down to read detailed survey responses.
  • Use the menu buttons at the top right to view the image gallery and take the survey yourself.

Created by Gia Kim (giaari15@g.ucla.edu) and Justine Lim (justineclim@g.ucla.edu). If you have any questions or need further assistance, feel free to reach out to us.
